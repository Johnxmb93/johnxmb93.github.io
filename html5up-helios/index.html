Hi. You're looking at Jonathan.

A hard working developer, leader, and friend (and your new potential hire). Below is my timeline

Early Years (Kinda boring)

I grew up in the suburbs of Chicago, moved around a bit until I landed in Round Lake. I started elementery school here and I went all the way up to high school while living here.

Growing up

I moved through high school with mostly honor and a few AP classes and I ended up graduating with a 3.89GPA. I was really good calculus but I never saw myself doing that for a living.

Post Highschool

I did what everyone told me I had to do and I went to college (community college). I did not know what I wanted to do and just decided psychology would be a good major.

My College Experience

I quickly learned college was not for everyone, myself included. I was miserable. I was doing poorly in classes, I hardly talked to anyone and was just eating cheap fast food.

My Early Work Experience

I also quickly learned college is not free, or cheap. I had to get a job and start paying off classes. I got a job at my local Wal-mart and started unloading truscks and stocking shelves.

Having Fun

After my first semester I realized I needed to hangout with friends, go out more and started working out. This lead to me enjoying myself and started hating college a bit less.
